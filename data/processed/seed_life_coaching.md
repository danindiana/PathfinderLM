# Seed Knowledge Base — Life Coaching Fundamentals

A tiny starter document so a fresh clone can run `python scripts/build_index.py`
immediately and exercise the RAG path. Replace or augment with your own corpus.

## Goal Setting

Effective goals are **SMART**: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and
Time-bound. Break large ambitions into the smallest possible next step — starting
is usually the hardest part, and a tiny first action builds momentum.

## Beating Procrastination

The Pomodoro Technique: work in focused 25-minute intervals separated by 5-minute
breaks. After four intervals, take a longer 15–30 minute break. Time-boxing makes
tasks feel manageable and reduces the urge to delay.

## Building Habits

Habits form through a cue → routine → reward loop. To build a new habit, anchor it
to an existing one (habit stacking), make the cue obvious, and make the reward
immediate. To break a bad habit, increase friction around the cue.

## Managing Stress

Brief, regular practices help most: box breathing (inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s,
hold 4s), a short daily walk, and a consistent sleep schedule. Name the emotion you
feel — labeling reduces its intensity.

## Reflection

End each day with three prompts: What went well? What drained me? What is the one
most important thing for tomorrow? Regular reflection turns experience into
learning.
